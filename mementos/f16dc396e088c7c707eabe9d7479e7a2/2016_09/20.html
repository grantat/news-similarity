    Bombing suspect charged after shootout
    Authorities: No sign suspect was part of larger network
    A swift manhunt culminated in a gun battle and the arrest of Ahmad Khan Rahami in connection with bombings in Manhattan and Seaside Park, N.J.
    Rahami was charged with attempted murder of a law enforcement officer in the shootout with police in Linden, N.J. A federal official said terrorism charges are also expected.
    Officials said they do not believe Rahami operated as part of a terrorist cell, but they were working to determine his motivation and whether anyone helped.
