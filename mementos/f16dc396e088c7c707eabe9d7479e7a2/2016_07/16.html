    The embattled government struggled to maintain control as supporters of President Recep Tayyip Erdogan and his ruling party took to the streets in several major cities. Erdogan spoke via FaceTime and urged people to oppose the coup.
    President Obama and Secretary of State John F. Kerry voiced support for Turkey’s government and urged restraint to avoid bloodshed.
