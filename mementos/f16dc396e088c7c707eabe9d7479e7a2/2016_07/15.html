    Incident being treated as an attack; motive unknown
    The truck rammed into a crowd celebrating Bastille Day in the French Riviera city of Nice, killing at least 75 people and injuring more than 50 as the driver also opened fire on revelers, French officials said.
    The driver was shot to death by police, and the truck was said to be carrying arms and explosives when it struck the crowd at about 10:30 p.m. local time.
