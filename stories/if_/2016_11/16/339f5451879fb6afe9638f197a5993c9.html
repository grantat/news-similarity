Trump's ex-wife Marla Maples spotted at Trump Tower

  • Comment
  • Share
  • Tweet
  • Stumble
  • Email

Donald Trump’s ex-wife and Tiffany Trump’s mother, Marla Maples, was spotted at Trump Tower in New York City Tuesday. 

Maples is an American actress and television personality, widely known for her affair and subsequent six-year marriage to the President-elect Trump.

The couple was married in December 1993 at New York City’s Plaza Hotel, in a ceremony attended by 1,000 guests.

They separated in May 1997 and divorced in 1999. They have one child together, Tiffany. 

It is unclear why Maples was at Trump Tower.
