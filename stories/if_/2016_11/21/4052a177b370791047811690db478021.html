AMAs 2016: List of winners at the American Music Awards

0
  • Andrea Park
  • CBS News

Last Updated Nov 20, 2016 11:24 PM EST

Rapper Drake was up for the most awards at the 2016 AMAs, with 13 nominations. 

  • AMAs 2016: Highlights and winners at the American Music Awards

Gigi Hadid and Jay Pharoah hosted the awards show, which included performers by Bruno Mars and the Chainsmokers.
