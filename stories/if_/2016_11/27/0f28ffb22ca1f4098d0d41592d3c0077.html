'DWTS' pro Mark Ballas marries BC Jean

Jayme Deerwester , USA TODAY 6:23 p.m. EST November 26, 2016
1.0k Shares

Congrats to the happy couple. (Photo: Vivien Killilea)

Another Dancing With the Stars pro is off the market.

Two-time winner and former USA TODAY blogger Mark Ballas married his bandmate BC Jean, aka Brittney Jean Carlson, Friday in a ceremony not far from  Los Angeles.

“Your wedding is something you subconsciously prepare for your entire life," he told People, which will feature photos from their nuptials in next week's issue. "You wonder who it’s going to be with, where it is going to be and how the ceremony and reception are going to be. Now it’s finally here and happening. It’s surreal. I just want to soak it all up because I just know I’m going to click my fingers and the whole evening will be over and I want to make sure I enjoy it.”

Ballas is currently sitting this season out in favor of a stint on Broadway. He's the last actor to portray Frankie Valli in Jersey Boys, which is scheduled to close on Jan. 15.
