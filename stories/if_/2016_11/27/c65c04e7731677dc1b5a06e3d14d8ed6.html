South Florida mall shooting: 1 dead, 1 injured

  • Comment
  • Share
  • Tweet
  • Stumble
  • Email

CORAL SPRINGS, Fla. -- Police locked down a South Florida mall after one person was killed and another was injured in a shooting on Saturday morning, CBS Miami reports.

Detectives said the shooting took place in one of the stores inside the Coral Square Mall, located in suburban Fort Lauderdale.

The mall, which has 120 stores, went on lockdown around 10:30 a.m. and it reopened by around 11:05 a.m. Authorities said they’re investigating.

Coral Springs police said on Twitter that no suspects were being sought. By late Saturday morning, police were not looking for an active shooter and the area was secure.

Authorities have not said what led to the shooting.
