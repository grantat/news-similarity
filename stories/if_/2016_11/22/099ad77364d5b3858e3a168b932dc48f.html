Report: Cutler could be out for season

The Chicago Bears may have taken another serious hit.

Quarterback Jay Cutler has a shoulder injury that could put his season in jeopardy, according to the Chicago Tribune. Cutler missed five games earlier this season with a thumb injury.

With Brian Hoyer on injured reserve, Matt Barkley would likely take over as the starting quarterback.

Fitzpatrick back as starter

FLORHAM PARK, N.J. — The Jets are sticking with Ryan Fitzpatrick as their starting quarterback.

Coach Todd Bowles announced the decision this afternoon after practice.

The decision is sure to anger some fans, because the Jets are 3-7 and out of the playoff race. Fitzpatrick may give the Jets the best chance to win with his experience, though at this point, winning only hurts the Jets' draft position.

But apparently, the Jets aren't ready to turn the team over the the 25-year-old Bryce Petty — or they don't think he's ready for that just yet.

The Jets return from their bye week today, to start getting ready for Sunday's game against the Patriots.

— Andy Vasquez, The (Bergen, N.J.) Record

Bears LB Freeman suspended four games

Chicago Bears linebacker Jerrell Freeman was suspended four games Monday for violating the NFL's policy on performance-enhancing substances.

Bears wide receiver Alshon Jeffery also is serving a four-game suspension for the same infraction.

Freeman leads the Bears with 91 tackles this season.
