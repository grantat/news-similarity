Trey Gowdy mocks his hair in new campaign ad

Mary Troyan , USA Today 6:56 p.m. EDT November 4, 2016
32 Shares

Rep. Trey Gowdy (R-S.C.) speaks at the National Rifle Association's NRA-ILA Leadership Forum during the NRA Convention at the Kentucky Exposition Center on May 20, 2016 in Louisville, Kentucky. (Photo: Scott Olson, Getty Images)

WASHINGTON -- Rep. Trey Gowdy delivers some expert-level self-deprecating humor in a new campaign ad filmed in a barbershop.

Gowdy, a Republican running for a fourth term from Upstate South Carolina's 4th District, is being challenged by Chris Fedalei, a Democrat who is also from Spartanburg.

With the tag line "consistent conservative, inconsistent haircuts," Gowdy mocks his own ever-changing hairstyles, from youthfully spiky to the Draco Malfoy slick back.

Gowdy's hair has even inspired a parody Twitter account and a Buzzfeed profile: This Man Has the Most Confusing Hair in Congress.

The 30-second spot ends with an unusual disclaimer: "This is Trey Gowdy, and for some reason, I approved this message."
