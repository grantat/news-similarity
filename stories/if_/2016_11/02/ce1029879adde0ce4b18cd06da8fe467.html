With the Cubs finally reaching the World Series, fans had a lot of celebrating to do — and while they were busy partying, they turned to TaskRabbit to mount big new TVs, hold spots at bars and do Chick-fil-A runs.

TaskRabbit, the San Francisco-based online service that lets people hire out quick jobs from cleaning to delivery, shared 10 Cubs-related requests listed on the site since Oct. 15. While the Cubs were at Wrigley last weekend, the company said, it booked 57 percent more cleaning tasks in Chicago than any other weekend last month. It also saw a 40 percent spike in deliveries on Saturday night.
