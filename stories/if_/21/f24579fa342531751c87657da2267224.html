Ciara's baby bump gets red-carpet debut at the AMAs

Carly Mallenbaum , USA TODAY 7:44 p.m. EST November 20, 2016
34 Shares

Ciara's baby bump got its red-carpet debut at the Microsoft Theatre. (Photo: Steve Granitz, WireImage)

Ciara wasn't shy about showing her pregnant belly at the American Music Awardss red carpet Sunday night in Los Angeles.

The singer, who announced last month that she and husband Russell Wilson are expecting their first child together, showed off her bump in a black long-sleeve gown that hugged her midsection.

While touching her belly, Ciara showed off her wedding bling. She and Wilson were married in July. (Photo: Frederick M. Brown, Getty Images)

The last time Ciara was pregnant on the AMAs red carpet, she wasn't so obvious about it. Ciara tweeted a throwback photo of her outfit from 2013, when she was expecting a child with ex Future.
