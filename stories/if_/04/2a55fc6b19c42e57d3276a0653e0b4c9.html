Jenner wore not one but two outfits over the course of the night USA TODAY

Kendall Jenner had quite a birthday night.

The supermodel became the latest Kardashian-Jenner to turn 21, splitting her time between two L.A. hotspots as she celebrated finally being able to drink. (Legally.)

vintage Paris Hilton vibes. 21 21.

A photo posted by Kendall (@kendalljenner) on Nov 3, 2016 at 8:24am PDT

First, she and her family visited West Hollywood's Catch LA, with Kendall sporting a Julien Macdonald lace-up catsuit for the occasion.

You only turn 21 once! (Photo: Julien Macdonald)

One costume change later, and she was partying with her celebrity friends at Delilah (a favorite of Drake and Katy Perry), with names like Tyga, Hailey Baldwin, Caitlyn Jenner, Jordyn Woods, Cindy Crawford, Gabrielle Union, Paris Hilton, Jonathan Cheban and Travis Scott reportedly in attendance.

Kendall wore a custom LaBourjoisie dress, made of Swarovski crystals and silver mesh, which she paired with Kenneth Cole Kam Sneakers.

And her big present of the night? A new car. Not bad for 21.
