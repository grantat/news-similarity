Make-Ahead Thanksgiving Recipes
Dishes you can make in advance, so you can focus on the turkey (and your guests) on the big day. Pie crust, stock, bread as well as most cakes, casseroles and relishes can be made weeks in advance and frozen. Freeze unbaked yeast bread or biscuit dough then bake off when you're ready.
