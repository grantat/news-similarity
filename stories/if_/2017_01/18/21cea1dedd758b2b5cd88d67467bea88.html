    'Fox & Friends' Wednesday: Ainsley Earhardt Interviews the President-Elect

    share this
    email
    As seen on Fox & Friends
    by Fox News Insider
    Posted in: 
    • // Trump transition
    • // Donald Trump

    Ainsley Earhardt will have a must-see sit-down with President-elect Donald Trump, Wednesday on "Fox & Friends."

    Earhardt interviewed Mr. Trump at Trump Tower just days before his inauguration as the 45th President of the United States.

    She and Mr. Trump discussed his thoughts on the historic ceremony, the boycotts by Democrats, the recent comments by Rep. John Lewis’ (D-GA), his relationship with the press and what he plans to do on his first day in office.

    Ainsley Earhardt will be on "Hannity" tonight at 10pm ET to give viewers a preview of the big interview. Make sure to watch it Wednesday morning, 6am to 9am ET on "Fox & Friends!"
