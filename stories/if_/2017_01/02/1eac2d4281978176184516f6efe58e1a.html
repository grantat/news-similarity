Aqib Talib ripped Michael Crabtree’s gold chain right off his neck and didn't get penalized

By: Andrew Joseph | January 1, 2017 5:03 pm Follow @andyj0seph

Every NFL game has its share of arguments, but I don’t think I’ve ever seen this happen during a scuffle.

Broncos cornerback Aqib Talib exchanged words with the Raiders’ Michael Crabtree near the sidelines. He then proceeded to rip Crabtree’s gold chain right off his neck.

Really, he broke the necklace completely apart.

Crabtree understandably wasn’t happy with Talib because who does that? The two continued to exchange words from across the field.

Aqib Talib yanks Michael Crabtree's gold chain right off of his neck.

[CBS] pic.twitter.com/Hy5p1TBgtQ

— Funhouse (@SportsFunhouse) January 1, 2017

When you wear jewelry while playing sports, you sort of set yourself up for it to break in some way. But that didn’t make Talib’s antics any less surprising.

Talib was not called for a penalty.
