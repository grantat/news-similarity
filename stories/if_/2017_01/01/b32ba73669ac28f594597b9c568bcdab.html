Dale Earnhardt Jr. is heading into 2017 as a married man.

The NASCAR driver and longtime girlfriend Amy Reimann are tying the knot on Saturday — an event that Earnhardt has been documenting the run up to on Twitter — from the day of:

Thanks for the well wishes and kind comments. @Amy_Reimann and I are very excited to be married today. Also, Happy New Year to everyone!

— Dale Earnhardt Jr. (@DaleJr) December 31, 2016
