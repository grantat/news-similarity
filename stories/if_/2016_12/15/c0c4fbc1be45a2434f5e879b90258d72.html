Is There Life Beneath Europa’s Oceans?

 

Europa is one of Jupiter’s moons and it may be the best chance to find life in our solar system. The surface of Europa is covered in ice but we know that liquid water is under that ice. And since water is essential for life, scientists think Europa is one of the most likely places in our solar system where life could possibly evolve.
