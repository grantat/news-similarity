Cop Who Shot Keith Lamont Scott 'Acted Lawfully,' DA Says

  • By Michael Edison Hayden
    Close
    Follow on Twitter
    More from Michael
Nov 30, 2016, 11:27 AM ET
  • 0 Shares
  • Email
  • Star
  • 0 Shares
  • Email

Brentley Vinson, the Charlotte-Mecklenburg police officer who shot Keith Lamont Scott, "acted lawfully," Mecklenburg County District Attorney Andrew Murray said today.

Criminal charges are not appropriate in this case, Murray added.

Scott's death in September sparked days of protest and unrest in Charlotte, North Carolina, after a series of videos of the encounter were released to the public.
