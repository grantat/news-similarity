Reports: Bradley Cooper, Irina Shayk expecting first child

Andrea Mandell , USA TODAY 9:09 p.m. EST November 30, 2016
Skip Ad
Ad Loading...
x

x

Irina Shayk is expecting her first child with Bradley Cooper. According to one insider, the pregnant star is in her second trimester and is "so excited" about becoming a mother for the very first time. USA TODAY

According to reports,Irina Shayk, who walked in the 2016 Victoria's Secret Fashion Show in Paris Wednesday, is pregnant.(Photo: Martin Bureau, AFP Getty Images)

Irina Shayk, 30, and Bradley Cooper, 41, are expecting their first child, according to reports.

E! News was first to report the model's pregnancy after Shayk strutted down the runway at the Victoria's Secret Fashion Show, held Wednesday in Paris. Us Weekly confirmed the report shortly after.

In one of her looks on the underwear-clad catwalk, Shayk kept her midriff slightly covered in a beaded fringed wrap.
