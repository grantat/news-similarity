See how Britney Spears marked her 35th birthday

Jayme Deerwester , USA TODAY 3:54 p.m. EST December 3, 2016

Friday's KIIS-FM Jingle Ball didn't just provide fans withan update on Kanye.

It also let them participate in Britney Spears' 35th birthday bash.

A giant bday cake was wheeled out following her set at Los Angeles' Stapes Center, which included Toxic, Slumber Party,Womanizer and I'm a Slave 4 U.

What did Britney wish for? (Photo: Chris Pizzello, Invision/AP)

Watch as Ryan Seacrest leads the arena in a tone-deaf chorus of Happy Birthday.

Thank you for the sweet birthday wishes! Had an amazing day. Love you all #KIISJingleBall
