Tiger Woods starts hot, deals with adversity to shoot 70 on Saturday

Steve DiMeglio , USA TODAY Sports 4:16 p.m. EST December 3, 2016

Tiger Woods lines up his putt on the first green during Round 3 of the Hero World Challenge.(Photo: Christian Petersen, Getty Images)

Much like his Thursday round, Tiger Woods got off to a hot start in the Bahamas.

Then he made the turn to the back nine. Woods shot a four-under 32 on the front but hit some adversity on the back of his third round at the Hero World Challenge.

USA TODAY

Tracking Tiger: Follow hole-by-hole look at Woods' Saturday round

Back-to-back bogeys on holes 13 and 14 were plagued with mishits off the tee, leading to a two-over 38on the back nine.

Woods currently sits at eight-under par heading into Sunday's final round.
