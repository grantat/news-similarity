By: Luke Kerr-Dineen | December 29, 2016 11:27 am Follow @LukeKerrDineen

Ronda Rousey is inching ever-nearer to her potentially career-defining showdown with Amanda Nunes. Getting handed a title shot in her first fight since losing to Holly Holm more than a year ago was a controversial move, but Rousey appears to be laser-focused on avenging herself.

She hasn’t been doing much media ahead of the fight, but she’s been actively sharing her progress on her official Instagram account. On Wednesday, she shared this picture of herself ahead of the Nunes fight, alongside a picture of herself from before the her bout against Cat Zingano. The difference, as you can see, is clear.

Picture on the left taken 5 days prior to #RouseyVsZingano Picture on right take 5 days prior to #RouseyVsNunes #FearTheReturn #FridayDec30 Pics by @jeffbottari

A photo posted by rondarousey (@rondarousey) on Dec 28, 2016 at 12:42pm PST
