Read the emails released by Emanuel in lawsuit settlement

  • Rahm Emanuel

Chicago Mayor Rahm Emanuel directed the city's Law Department and his personal attorney to settle a lawsuit brought by the Better Government Association. In settling the BGA lawsuit, Emanuel agreed to turn over about 2,700 pages of emails that his personal attorney determined were government-related in nature.
