Travel

From a portable tea set to a sketchpad that saves your in-flight doodles, these gifts are guaranteed to become indispensable for your jet-setter friends.

By Elaine Glusac and Shivani Vora

Teavana Tea Infuser

Pack the interior infuser with your favorite tea leaves and set off for the airport with Teavana’s poinsettia-patterned 16-ounce tumbler. Just add hot water, after going through security.

$33 Buy Now

Yatra Portable Waterproof Bluetooth Speaker

Amplify the back country (or your backyard) with the travel-ready Bluetooth speaker Aquatune 9612 from Yatra. Attach a carabiner, and the 7-inch waterproof speaker, which weighs less than one pound, clips to any backpack.

$70 Buy Now

Ecomo Smart Water Bottle

The new Ecomo is a smart water bottle that triple-filters metals, chemicals and bacteria from drinking water and syncs to your smartphone to track daily hydration. Recently funded via Kickstarter, the device will begin shipping pre-orders in March.

$139 Buy Now

LaMove Mobile Closet

The closet designer Lisa Adams has taken on the wheel-aboard bag. With a zip-out garment bag and dedicated compartments for shoes, toiletries and more, the case can be hung up without unpacking.

$575 Buy Now

The AirHook

Ever wonder where to securely mount your electronic device on a plane? The Airhook attaches to an upright tray table, providing a platform, with an anchoring strap to keep it stable, as well as a beverage cup holder.

$26.00 Buy Now

Bugaboo Boxer

This new luggage system aims to improve mobility. The hard-sided cases click into a wheeled trolley you push rather than drag, and the smaller wheel-aboard-size case includes an inner bag that doubles as a day pack.

$1,030 Buy Now

Wacom Bamboo Folio Smartpad

On this tablet-sized smartpad, you can sketch the Eiffel Tower on paper, then hit a button to convert it into a digital form you can edit. It also converts handwritten notes to text, and comes with its own pen and 5 GB of storage.

$191 Buy Now

Hastens Travel Pillow

This 15-inch square pillow, which contains 30 percent duck down and 70 percent duck feathers, was made for inflight naps. It has a central zip compartment for a passport, wallet or other valuables.

$170 Buy Now

BabyZen Yoyo+ Stroller

Why wait for your gate-checked stroller? BabyZen Yoyo+ strollers fold down to carry-on size. Models weigh around 14 pounds, include a shoulder strap for carrying, and have removable, machine-washable pads.

$495 Buy Now

Margherita Missoni Child's Backpack

This eye-catching backpack has enough room for favorite toys and snacks, making it an ideal carry-on. An Italian fashion designer dreamed it up for her son. Now, other kids can sport it, too.

$45 Buy Now

Backpacker: The Ultimate Travel Game

In this fast-paced card game, players compete against one another to return home from their globe-trotting with the most photos. Along the way, they have the chance to “visit” more than 38 countries and grapple with the unpredictability of traveling.

$17.50 Buy Now

Coffee Table Books from Assouline

Do you dream of skiing in Gstaad or soaking up the rich greenery in Bali? This set of coffee table books, full of lush imagery, may get you excited about doing both or, at the very least, temporarily transport you there.

$120 Buy Now

Every Mother Counts Tote Bag

Practical enough to carry toiletries yet polished enough to be a clutch, this pouch benefits more than your trip: all of the net sales proceeds go to Every Mother Counts, a women’s charity.

$45 Buy Now

Leather Tech Envelope by Mark and Graham

This buttery leather envelope has pockets and straps to hold chargers, cables, headphones, an iPad mini and even your passport. It also is slim enough to slip into any bag. Includes monogramming.

$149 Buy Now

Leica Sofort Instant Camera

Sometimes, old-school photography really does rule. Everyone from kids to adults will have fun using this instant camera that takes high-quality shots and prints them in seconds. And, even the most gadget-averse will find it easy to use. Available in three colors.

$299 Buy Now
