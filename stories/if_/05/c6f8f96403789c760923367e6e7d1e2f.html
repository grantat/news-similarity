40% of Millennials fear the election is rigged

By Holly Epstein Ojalvo 6:30 pm EDT November 4, 2016

31 shares
SHARE
TWEET
SMS
SEND
EMAIL

There’s a ton of talk about the presidential election being rigged.

.@realDonaldTrump: "The system's rigged. She shouldn't be allowed to run. Hillary has engaged in a massive criminal enterprise & cover-up." pic.twitter.com/9Q8G2N0cig

— Fox News (@FoxNews) November 4, 2016

Donald Trump calls this a rigged election, because it is – against Hillary. https://t.co/8hEmDkYg0f

— Barbra Streisand (@BarbraStreisand) November 4, 2016

And it turns out that a lot of people think it’s possible that the election actually is being rigged — especially Millenials.

According to a new poll released by web agency Rad Campaign and analytic research firm Lincoln Park Strategies, 40% of Millennials think the election is vulnerable to fradulent fixing. And more young people worry about voter suppression (45%) than about voter fraud (33%).

Hillary Clinton supporters and Donald Trump supporters are split on views about election tampering. (Rad Campaign)

If you’re one of the people concerned about election rigging or voter fraud, you can probably relax. “A person is more likely to be struck by lightning than for voter fraud to occur,” Allyson Kapin, the co-founder of Rad Campaign, said in a press release. “Voter fraud rarely happens. There have only been a handful of verified cases of ineligible voters attempting to cheat the election system.”

Related: No, you can’t vote for president on Twitter or Facebook

Voter suppression, on the other hand, does seem to be happening.

And there’s a marked difference between Hillary Clinton supporters and Donald Trump supporters when it comes to beliefs around election tampering.

“Donald Trump’s rhetoric around a ‘rigged’ election is not resonating with most Americans,” Stefan Hankin, founder of Lincoln Park Strategies, said in the release. “However, it is certainly working with his supporters. The fact that two-thirds of them feel the election could be rigged does not bode well for our democracy or our country.”
