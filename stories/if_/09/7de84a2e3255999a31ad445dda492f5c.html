Donald Trump cake spotted at Trump Tower, becomes instant Twitter fodder

Jesse Yomtov , USA TODAY 8:29 p.m. EST November 8, 2016
202 Shares
Skip Ad
Ad Loading...
x

Embed

x

Share

A cake created to resemble Donald Trump's bust was pictured and posted to social media, not long before polls started to close. The reaction to the cake was fast and memes began popping up almost immediately. USA TODAY NETWORK

A Donald Trump cake being wheeled into Trump Tower @abc#ElectionDaypic.twitter.com/HSkHChfUSA

— Jason Volack (@jasonvolack) November 8, 2016

A Donald Trump cake bust was spotted outside Trump Tower in Manhattan on Tuesday evening, where Trump said he will be watching the election results with family and friends.

Later, the cake was on display at Trump's Election Night party.

Here's the Trump cake -- a bust of Donald Trump -- at his Election Night party pic.twitter.com/gQjCR25S9Q

— Katherine Faulders (@KFaulders) November 9, 2016

Needless to say, Twitter had a field day:

how long til cake boss is fired in favor of whoever made that trump cake masterpiece

— Ivan Brandon (@IvanBrandon) November 8, 2016

Who's gonna put a #cryingjordan on #trumpcake's face?

— Daniel M. Jimenez (@DMJreports) November 8, 2016

Actual footage of them making the Trump cake: pic.twitter.com/fIufP5QvdF

— Life's Scary Enough (@BilgeEbiri) November 8, 2016

So, we all agree William H. Macy will get an Emmy nomination for his portrayal as the sad Trump cake in the HBO movie, right?

— Allan Mott (@HouseofGlib) November 8, 2016

Our friends at For The Win pointed out that the cake looks exactly like famed soccer coach Arsenal Wenger.

No word on the flavor(s) of the cake.
